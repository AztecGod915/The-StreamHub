Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last updated: June 27, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. We don't show you ads. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

The StreamHub ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered streaming discovery platform available at thestreamhub.app. We help users find what to watch across streaming services, track live sports scores, and manage their watchlists.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@thestreamhub.app

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Profile picture: If you choose to upload one
  • Streaming service preferences: Which services you subscribe to (e.g. Netflix, Hulu)
  • Watchlist: Movie and TV show IDs you save
  • Ratings and reviews: Star ratings and written reviews you submit
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Features you use, pages visited, and general interaction patterns
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and screen size
  • Local storage: App preferences and session data stored in your browser
  • Sports predictions: Game outcome predictions you make in the Sports Hub

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the StreamHub service
  • To personalize your experience (AI recommendations, For You picks, Watch Tonight)
  • To sync your watchlist, ratings, and preferences across devices
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send account-related emails (receipts, password resets)
  • To improve the app based on how people use it
  • To display your public profile if you choose to share it

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements. The StreamHub products are ad-free.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate The StreamHub:

Supabase

Our authentication and database provider. Your account credentials, watchlist, ratings, and profile data are stored securely in Supabase. Privacy Policy →

Stripe

Payment processing for Premium subscriptions. Stripe handles all payment data — we never store your card details. Privacy Policy →

Anthropic (Claude AI)

Powers our AI features: Mood Search, Watch Tonight, For You, and Cost Report. When you use these features, your preferences and service list are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models. Privacy Policy →

TMDB (The Movie Database)

Provides movie and TV show data, images, and metadata. Privacy Policy →

ESPN API

Provides live sports scores and schedules. No personal data is shared with ESPN.

Vercel

Our hosting provider. Vercel may log standard web request data (IP addresses, request times) for security and performance purposes. Privacy Policy →

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser cookies and local storage for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in between sessions
  • Preferences: Your streaming service selections and app settings
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to understand how the app is used
  • Consent: To remember your cookie preferences

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing cookies will log you out of your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law (e.g. payment records retained for 7 years for tax purposes).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your watchlist and ratings data
  • Objection: Opt out of any non-essential data processing

EU and UK residents have additional rights under GDPR. To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@thestreamhub.app.

8. Children's Privacy

The StreamHub is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
