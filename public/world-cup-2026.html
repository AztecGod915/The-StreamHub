❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the 2026 World Cup free to watch?
Yes — if you have a TV antenna you can watch Fox over the air for free. Tubi also streams full match replays for free. Fox One offers a 3-day free trial for all live matches in English.
What channel is the World Cup on?
In the US, Fox airs 70 matches and FS1 airs 34 matches in English. Spanish coverage is on Telemundo (92 matches) and Universo. All 104 matches are available through streaming services like Fubo, YouTube TV, and Fox One.
When does the 2026 World Cup end?
The Final is on July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium in New Jersey. The tournament runs from June 11 to July 19 with 104 total matches.
How many teams are in the 2026 World Cup?
48 teams for the first time ever — expanded from 32. The group stage has 12 groups of 4, and 32 teams advance to a new Round of 32 knockout stage.
